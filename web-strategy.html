Web Strategy

PPC, SEO, Analytics

Both as a department lead in a full-service agency setting and as an independent consultant, I’ve helped countless small to medium sized businesses execute strategies to deliver a return from the web. While my current role as Senior Strategist at Overit focuses primarily on web advertising—managing search, display, social, and video placements—my work experience includes search engine optimization, web analytics, and content writing as well.

My goal is to help business decision makers find the best mix of paid and earned media to reach their target audiences, while implementing the proper data tracking to evaluate success. I also possess a moderate knowledge of front-end web development, with a solid grasp of HTML/CSS, an ongoing effort to learn JavaScript, JQuery, and PHP, and extensive experience customizing sites built in templates such as WordPress.

Web-Related Skills & Experience

  • Managing monthly PPC (pay-per-click) advertising campaigns with monthly budgets as high as $30k and as low as $1k through Google AdWords, Bing Ads, Facebook Advertising, Twitter Ads, and other platforms
  • Implementing analytics to track website activity and conversions, as well as evaluating visitor data to monitor site performance and make recommendations for improvement. I’m well-versed in Google Analytics but do work with other tools, such as KISSmetrics.
  • Split testing webpage and ad variations to determine best performance and usability
  • Overseeing SEO (search engine optimization) strategy, both through consultation and hands-on implementation
  • Writing content for blogs, informational pages, how-to guides, and other sources
  • Setting up websites in WordPress, including extensive front-end customization, as well as optimization for search engine performance
  • Building basic websites outside of a content management system utilizing HTML/CSS frameworks, in addition to basic JavaScript and JQuery.
